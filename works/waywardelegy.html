WAYWARD ELEGY

Note to self: Replace banner with story logo.

Wayward Elegy is an audio drama (or comic, still deciding but leaning to audio drama) inspired by the Sonic the Hedgehog and Megaman X universes, combined into their own unique story about Mercenary furries. The titular story of this website, and probably my first release ever!
The story focuses on two rival Mercenaries: Elegy, a Jackal from Mountain Top, and Blade, a Tasmanian Devil from the dying island of Forest Bay. It details their journey from rivals to companions, and the conflict that brought the two together.

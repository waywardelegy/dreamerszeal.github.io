TO TIME'S END

Note to self: Replace banner with story logo.

To Time's End is an audio drama inspired by Chrono Trigger and my own personal spirituality, combined into a story about saving the world from danger by forgiving those who wronged you.
The story focuses mainly on three figures: Aera, Luca, and Leena, a trio of kids from a kingdom in a distant land. It details their journey to stop a mysterious monster from destroying their world-- and their journey to come to terms with things about themselves and their relationships.

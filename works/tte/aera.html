Aera Merrid Diem

he/they | tabby | fire elemental


art placeholder

The protagonist. A hero from a small village, he was found upon the doorstep of a young woman in a small village; and she raised him as if he were her own son.

Best friends with Luca Atenash, he contributed in part to the chaos that they caused throughout the village.
Aera is combative, but trusting. He spends his time planning out long adventures he may never truly go on, and helping Luca with their experiments.

He has a bit of a phobia of heights.

Aera has a history unknown to him, sharing the name of a mysterious being who controls time throughout the World. Over the course of his adventure, he starts to lose and gain some bits of his identity...
